(Signal Corps Photo). Adams, 3234 Sig Photo Det. Okinawa. Higa, Kamado, age 63, Okinawan snake catcher from Nakao displaying the deadly snake Habu found in Okinawa. In catching a snake he uses a hollow piece of bamboo with a heavy piece of wire with a curve on the end which runs through the center of bamboo. He has been catching these snakes for 20 yrs and was paid 20 yen for each snake by the Jap govt. The Habu snake is sometimes as long as 7ft. S Sgt Mokis, Photo Asgmt Det 28 (Signal Corps Photo).
